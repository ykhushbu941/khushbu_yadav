Scope of Work
* Game Development: Implement three core puzzle types (Sudoku, Word Search, Sliding Tile) with dynamic generation and rigorous mathematical validation to ensure solvability.
* Full-Stack Architecture: Develop a decoupled frontend (React/Vue) and backend (Node/Python) to manage user accounts, leaderboards, and persistent state.
* Security & Performance: Integrate secure password hashing, server-side score validation to prevent cheating, and ensure sub-2-second load times across mobile and desktop.
